Course Title : Life and Community in Urban World
Course Code : H 238 (Debashish Pattnaik)
Credits : 4
Course Category : Elective
Course Prerequisite : None
Contact Hours : 28
Course Outcome:
  • Analyze urbanization processes and their contribution to human wellbeing.
  • Understand how and why different forms of urbanization in countries have developed, and their implications for population and society.
  • Understand the socio-political conditions of metropolitan areas and their impacts on individuals, institutions and society
  • Understand the relationship between planning, property development and urban growth.

Course Contents:

  1. Introduction, Urban space and urban thought (3 classes)
  2. Urbanization, Emergence and evolution of cities & Urban life (5 classes + 1 tutorial)
  3. Urban communities, Communities online and offline (4 classes)
  4. The city in context, Migration, Sorting and niches, (4 classes + 1 tutorial)
  5. Markets & networks, City infrastructure, Growth & management (3 classes)
  6. Suburbia, Urban ecology (3 classes)
  7. Urban design, Policy and planning (3 classes + tutorial)
